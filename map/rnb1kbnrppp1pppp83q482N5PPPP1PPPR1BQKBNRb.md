From here, Black essentially has 3 choices
as to which kind of Scandi they want to go into.
The main line, the Valencia, and the Gubinsky-Melts
are actually fairly different from each other
in terms of the setup that Black is going for.
Characteristic to the Scandi, though,
is that White can play normal developing moves against each
for the most part, and not randomly die or whatever.
The Classical Scandi isn't usually super sharp for White.
