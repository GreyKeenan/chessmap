---

The Englund Gambit is ultimately considered unsound at high levels.
However, it gives Black practical chances since,
in order to punish it, White has to accept and play into Black's theory.

Declining is an option as well (for huge cowards).
I do not decline it, and I do not play it,
so you won't find notes on those variations here.
